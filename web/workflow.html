How LegisTracerEU Works

From EC reference lists to verified verbatim quotations in seconds — a practical guide to integrating EU law search into your legal translation workflow.

The Core Requirement: Verbatim Precision

Translating for EU institutions is not like general translation. When the European Commission assigns a project, it provides a mandatory list of reference documents — specific legislative acts identified by their Celex numbers — that must be consulted throughout the translation. Quotations from legal acts must match the official published text word for word. Paraphrase is a quality defect.

What this means in practice: During a typical assignment, a translator may need to locate and verify dozens of precise citations across multiple legal acts — often under time pressure. Each search costs time. Each misquote costs quality score points. The only way to maintain both accuracy and speed is fast, scoped, full-text search directly in the required reference corpus.

LegisTracerEU is built specifically for this use case: give it the EC reference list, and it searches inside it — instantly, in any EU language combination.

The Workflow, Step by Step

1

Load Your Reference Set

Enter the Celex numbers from the EC reference list in the Upload References tab. The app fetches and indexes those acts under a named Custom Collection. All searches are then scoped to exactly those documents.

2

Search in Seconds

Enter a phrase — a term, a sentence fragment, or a partial quote. The app searches the full text of all reference documents and returns results immediately, ranked by relevance, highlighted in context.

3

Copy the Verbatim Text

Each result shows the surrounding passage in the official language version. Confirm it is the right reference, copy the exact wording, and insert it into your translation. EUR-Lex link included for verification.

4

Trados Works With You

The Trados Studio plugin fires a search automatically when you open a segment. Results appear beside your translation without any manual action — keeping pace as you work through the file.

Search Speed and Modes

The difference between 20 seconds and 3 minutes per citation compounds across hundreds of segments. LegisTracerEU returns results in under a second from tens of thousands of EU legal documents, full-text across all official languages.

Phrase Search

Finds exact or near-exact matches for your phrase within the full text of EU legal acts. Ideal when you know the phrasing exists verbatim somewhere in the reference corpus and need to locate it fast.

  • Exact sentence and clause retrieval
  • Results displayed with surrounding legal context
  • Works in any EU official language
  • Results ranked by match quality

Fuzzy Search

Handles minor variations, typos, and partially recalled phrases. Searches across both source and target language in parallel — useful when the formulation is uncertain or when exploring terminology options.

  • Tolerates minor text variation
  • Cross-lingual parallel matching
  • Good for terminology discovery
  • Adjustable match threshold

Custom Collections: The EC Reference List Workflow

Every EC translation contract comes with a reference list: specific legislative acts, identified by Celex numbers, which the translator is required to follow. Quotations drawn from these acts must appear verbatim in the translation. Custom Collections in LegisTracerEU are designed precisely for this scenario.

How it works: In the Upload References tab, enter the Celex numbers from the EC reference list (comma-separated). Give the collection a name — for example Assignment_2025_271. The app downloads and indexes those acts. Thereafter, all searches run against exactly that corpus, returning only results relevant to your current project.
  • Create up to three named collections per account
  • Switch between collections or the full EU corpus with one click
  • Add new documents to a collection at any time as the assignment evolves
  • Results are limited to your project corpus — no noise from unrelated legislation
  • Each collection is private to your account, accessible from desktop app or Trados plugin
Why this matters: Searching the full body of EU law for a common phrase returns thousands of hits. Searching your project-specific reference set — the 30 or 40 acts the EC actually requires — returns the handful that apply. Faster to review, faster to cite, easier to audit against EC requirements.

Deep Integration with Trados Studio

The LegisTracerEU plugin for Trados Studio 2022 and later is signed by RWS (the Trados publisher) and installs in one step. It becomes part of your translation environment — not a separate tool requiring window-switching.

Automatic Segment Search

When you navigate to a segment in Trados Studio, the plugin reads the segment content and immediately fires a search against your active Custom Collection or the full corpus. Results appear in the LegisTracerEU panel — no keystrokes required.

  • Zero extra clicks for search per segment
  • Results keep pace as you move through the file
  • Panel can be docked anywhere in the Studio layout
  • Works with both source and target segment content

Manual Search Options

Select any text in the source segment, right-click and choose LegisTracerEU Search, or assign a keyboard shortcut (e.g. Alt+Down Arrow) in Trados Studio options. Useful for searching specific sub-phrases or individual terms without triggering a full segment search.

  • Right-click context menu in the editor
  • Configurable keyboard shortcut
  • Type any phrase directly in the results panel

Results in Context

Every result shows the surrounding passage in the official language version — not just the matching sentence. You see what comes before and after, enough to confirm the citation is correct and the formulation is the one that belongs in your translation.

  • Contextual passage shown around each hit
  • Match text highlighted for instant scanning
  • Direct link to the full document on EUR-Lex
  • Expandable context view for longer passages

IATE: EU Official Terminology

IATE (Interactive Terminology for Europe) is the interinstitutional terminology database of the European Union — used by the Commission, Parliament, Council and other EU bodies as the authoritative source of approved terminology. LegisTracerEU integrates IATE search directly alongside EUR-Lex law search.

  • All 24 EU official languages — same session, same interface
  • Domain-classified entries: legal, financial, technical, environmental, agricultural, institutional and more
  • Definitions, usage notes, and reliability indicators where available
  • Consistent with terminology decisions used by EC translators and revisers
  • Search IATE for a term, then switch to EUR-Lex to find it used in context — in the same workflow

Privacy and NDA Compliance

EU translation contracts regularly include strict non-disclosure obligations. LegisTracerEU is designed with legal confidentiality in mind — no part of your source documents or translation content ever leaves your machine or reaches a third-party service.

  • Search queries are processed on the server and immediately discarded — no query logging
  • Source documents and translation content are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere
  • No public AI systems, no external cloud services involved in any search operation
  • All results come exclusively from publicly available EU data (EUR-Lex, IATE)
  • Suitable for NDA-governed and confidential translation projects
