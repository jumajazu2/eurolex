Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Data Collection and Usage

Last updated: October 6, 2025

What data we collect:

  • Search queries: Temporarily processed for search functionality, then immediately discarded
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store payment data)
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous usage statistics via Google Analytics
  • IP address: For security and quota management purposes

Cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: For payment processing and service functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics for improving our service
  • Stripe cookies: For secure payment processing

Your rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification (correction) of your data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Data retention:

Search queries are processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We do not store or log your search content. Payment information is handled exclusively by Stripe according to their privacy policy.

Third-party services:
